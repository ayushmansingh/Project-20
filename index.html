Getting to Gwalior

Travel

Gwalior is easy to reach by air, rail and road — and every celebration happens at one address.

✈

By air

Gwalior Airport (GWL) is ~10 km from the city. Indore, Bhopal and Delhi are the nearest large hubs with onward connections.

❖

By train

Gwalior Junction sits on the main Delhi–Chennai line, with fast connections from Delhi (~3–4 hrs) and Agra (~1.5 hrs).

❀

By road

An easy, scenic drive from Agra (~120 km) and Delhi (~320 km) on the expressway, right up to the resort.
